Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Fostering creativity and technological advancements to drive economic growth and address social challenges.

Sustainability

Promoting environmental preservation and resource management to ensure long-term ecological balance and resilience.

Infrastructure

Building and upgrading physical and digital infrastructure to enhance connectivity, productivity, and quality of life.

Empowerment

Strengthening individuals and communities, particularly women and marginalized groups, to access opportunities, education, and equal rights.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Modern Infrastructure

"I dream of a world where poverty is eradicated, where the work of human beings is for the benefit of humanity, and where each person can live with dignity and hope.I see a Bangladesh where poverty is no longer a source of suffering, but a memory. A Bangladesh where every individual, no matter their background, has the opportunity to live with dignity, free from the constraints of poverty. This will be a Bangladesh where the power of entrepreneurship, especially social entrepreneurship, is harnessed to create solutions for the most vulnerable."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
